Mr. Storyteller

Mr. Storyteller

Brief / Design a new innovative smart product
Agency / Avans University Breda
My roles / Concepting, product design
Team / Yannic, Yenaz, Esther, Rianne

From a young age the new generation is almost glued to their screens. We can try keeping them away, but what if we can use them to get parent and child closer instead?

Remember back in the day when you would lay in your bed all tucked up. Your parent or guardian holds your favorite book and tells the story of a bear trying to find honey. You point at a drawing and smile.

Mr. Storyteller is a smart cover for these children books. After selecting the story you are reading, it starts listening to your voice. The screen on the cover shows a live animation.

Design process

Concepting
Technology is taking over, but books still exist. Reading a screen simply does not compare to those classic pages. Our project started with the question: how can we preserve this experience, but add smart technology to improve it?

Technical possibilities
The idea for a cover that animates a story brought up a new question: Can we create a live animation from sound? Pre-animated trigger words won't fit every story. An interface where you select the book you're reading is necessary.

Product design
A book is foldable, so our cover should be as well to preserve the right experience. Since displays are not yet foldable, our first prototype consists of two screens.

Prototyping
Different sizes books should be able to fit the cover. During a car ride a phone holder inspired me; Hooks kept the phone in place, so why not use those to hold a book?

Branding
By adding the hooks to the cover, they started to look like literal arms and legs. Jokingly we drew a smiley face and realized it became a little character. We gave him a hat and Mr. Storyteller was born.
